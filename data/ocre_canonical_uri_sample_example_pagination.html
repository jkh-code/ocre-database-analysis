Typological Description

  • Date Range: AD 330 - AD 331
  • Object Type: Coin
  • Manufacture: Struck
  • Denomination: AE2 (uncertain)
  • Denomination: AE3 (uncertain)
  • Material: Bronze

  • Authority

    • Authority: Constantine I

    • Geographic

      • Mint: Trier
      • Region: Gallia

    • Obverse

      • Legend: CONSTAN-TINOPOLIS
      • Type: Bust of Constantinopolis, laureate, helmeted, wearing imperial cloak, left, holding reversed spear in right hand
      • Deity: Constantinopolis

    • Reverse

      • Type: Victory, winged, draped, standing left on prow, holding spear in right hand and shield in left hand
      • Deity: Victory
      • MintMark: -/-//TRP•
      • OfficinaMark: P
      • OfficinaMark: S

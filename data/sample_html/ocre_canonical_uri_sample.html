Typological Description

  • Date Range: 25 BC - 23 BC
  • Object Type: Coin
  • Manufacture: Struck
  • Denomination: Quinarius
  • Material: Silver

  • Authority

    • Authority: Augustus
      • Issuer: P. Carisius

      • Geographic

        • Mint: Emerita
        • Region: Lusitania

      • Obverse

        • Legend: AVGVST
        • Type: Head of Augustus, bare, left
        • Portrait: Augustus

        • Reverse

          • Legend: P CARISI LEG
          • Type: Victory standing right, placing wreath on trophy with dagger and sword at base
          • Deity: Victory
